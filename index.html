Niccolò di Bernardo dei Machiavelli

"Everyone sees what you appear to be, few experience who you really are."

Niccolò di Bernardo dei Machiavelli was an Italian diplomat, politician, historian, philosopher, humanist, and writer of the Renaissance period. He has often been called the father of modern political science.

  • Born: May 3, 1469, Florence, Italy
  • Sun Sign: Taurus
  • Famous As: Political Philosopher
  • Died: June 21, 1527, Florence, Italy
    Popular Books Written
  1. The Prince
  2. The Art of War
  3. Discourses of Livy
